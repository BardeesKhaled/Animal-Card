Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals in the world.The largest elephant on record was an adult male African elephant.It weighed about 24,000 pounds and was 13 feet tall at the shoulder.

  • Length: 5.5 – 6.5 m
  • Height: 3.3 m – 2.7 m
  • Lifespan:60 – 70 years
  • Mass:5400 kg – 6,000 kg

Elephants have characteristic long noses, or trunks; large, floppy ears; and wide, thick legs. There are two species of elephant. The Asian elephant and the African elephant live on separate continents and have many unique features.
